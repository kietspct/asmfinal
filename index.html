Cooking Recipe: Grilled Ribs

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds pork ribs
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix barbecue sauce, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush ribs with sauce mixture, covering all sides.
  4. Grill ribs for 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally, until fully cooked and sauce caramelizes.
  5. Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
